🎁 Inspiration for Crafters

What Can You
Create?

Turn beautiful designs into treasured handmade gifts. Here are 9 project ideas for every type of crafter.

🍽️

Embroidered Kitchen Towels

Terry cloth and linen kitchen towels are a classic canvas. A floral design or sweet quote transforms everyday linen into a luxurious gift. Use a tear-away stabilizer with a water-soluble topper for best results.

Shop Floral Designs →
👜

Personalized Tote Bags

Canvas totes with embroidered monograms or illustrated designs are practical, eco-friendly and utterly charming. Add her name or a meaningful quote for an extra personal touch.

Shop Quote Designs →
🔆

Laser Engraved Wood Gifts

Personalized wooden plaques, signs, cutting boards and keepsake boxes. Use our laser cutting files with your Glowforge, xTool, or Sculpfun for stunning engraved gifts she'll display forever.

Shop Laser Files →
🖼️

Framed Hoop Art

Leave your embroidery right in the wooden hoop and hang it as wall art. Instantly chic, inexpensive to make, and the most personal home decor gift possible. Add a ribbon for a gift-ready finish.

Shop Modern Designs →
🛋️

Embroidered Throw Pillows

An embroidered pillow with a heartfelt quote is a gift that decorates her home and warms her heart every single day. Choose a design that matches her decor — florals for classic, modern minimal for contemporary.

Shop Quote Designs →
💌

Beautiful Greeting Cards

Use our clipart graphics to design stunning, professional-looking Mother's Day cards in Canva, Photoshop, or any design software. Print at home or through a local print shop for gorgeous results.

Shop Clipart →
👩‍🍳

Personalized Apron

A personalised apron is one of the most-used and best-loved gifts for a cooking mom. Embroider her name, a fun quote, or a whimsical design on the bib front. She'll think of you every time she cooks!

Shop Funny Designs →
👶

Baby Onesies & Blankets

Celebrate new mamas with sweet embroidered onesies, burp cloths, and swaddle blankets. These make incredibly touching baby shower gifts. Use water-soluble stabilizer on soft knit fabrics.

Shop New Mom Designs →
🎀

Keepsake Ornaments & Tags

Small laser-cut wood ornaments with personalized engravings are perfect keepsakes. They double as gift tags, bag charms, or Christmas ornaments she'll keep for decades. Quick to make, impossible to forget.
