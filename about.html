Our Story

Made with Love,
for Mom

Auracrafts was born from a simple belief: the best gifts are made by hand, and everyone deserves beautiful designs to create them with.

Our Mission

Helping Crafters Create Unforgettable Moments

Every year, Mother's Day comes around and millions of people want to create something truly special — something that says "I love you" far better than anything bought from a shelf. That's where we come in.

Auracrafts is a curated catalog of digital craft designs specifically chosen for Mother's Day. We've spent months hand-picking the best embroidery files, laser cutting designs, and graphics from Creative Fabrica's massive library — so you don't have to search through thousands of results.

Every design we feature has been evaluated for quality, compatibility, and genuine emotional impact. We look for designs that will make your finished gift feel treasured rather than just functional.

Whether you're an experienced embroiderer, a new laser cutter owner, or just someone who wants to print a beautiful card — you'll find exactly what you need here.

Privacy & Data

Privacy Policy

Data We Collect

Auracrafts does not collect personal data from visitors beyond standard anonymous analytics. If you subscribe to our newsletter, we store your email address to send you updates. We do not sell, rent, or share your email with third parties.

Your wishlist is stored in your browser's localStorage — on your device only. We have no access to your saved designs. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser's site data.

We use Google Fonts for typography (privacy policy: fonts.google.com/about). Links to Creative Fabrica and Amazon are tracked by their respective affiliate programs. Product images are hosted by Creative Fabrica and Unsplash. We may use Google Analytics for anonymous traffic analysis.
